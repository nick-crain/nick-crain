My work typically sits at the intersection of systems engineering, automation, integrations, and operational reliability.

Focus:

## Engineering Values

```text
Clarity over cleverness.
Reliability over shortcuts.
Documentation over tribal knowledge.
Automation over repetitive manual work.
Small, reviewable changes over risky rewrites.
```

---

## Collaboration Style

I try to keep my work practical, transparent, and easy to maintain.

| Principle | What That Means |
|---|---|
| **Communicate early** | Surface blockers, tradeoffs, and assumptions before they become problems. |
| **Leave context** | Write useful PR descriptions, comments, and documentation. |
| **Prefer simple systems** | Build solutions that are easy to understand, operate, and extend. |
| **Respect production** | Treat reliability, security, and observability as part of the work. |
| **Improve continuously** | Look for small ways to make systems, tools, and workflows better. |
